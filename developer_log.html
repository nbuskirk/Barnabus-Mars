Barnabus Mars

The story so far..

While studying new microbes found on a mars satellite, Galactically-renowned scientist Dr. Barnabus Mars, falls violently ill to a mysterious toxin. Barnabus awakens on the surface of Mars with no tools other than scientific instruments used to unearth Martian space parts, or alien artifacts. You must find a way off the Red Planet.
